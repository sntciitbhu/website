    The Grand Pitch Challenge

    Grand Pitch Challenge is the hub of project initiation in the institute. From drones to app-based B-Plans, innovators from every aspect compete on a single platform.

    Fake Investors

    Innovations are pitched in front of a panel consisting of experienced members of the club. It may act as a qualifier for the GPC.

    Brainstorming Sessions

    Such sessions, in the form of face-offs or group discussions, are held regularly to boost up the critical thinking of innovators. Brainstorming takes place over a particular technology or a project idea.
