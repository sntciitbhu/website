About | Astronomy Club

The astronomy club is a part of the Science and Technology Council(SnTC) in which we explore the winsome and ravishing night sky with the help of dazzling telescopes.Down from the subatomic and theorized particles to the massive super clusters you can be damn sure the astronomy club has got all the 62 orders of magnitude covered.The utmost zeal of the club is to bring out the passion for astronomy in every individual through various theoretical and observational workshops.The club offers a plethora of various activities to engage in–from updating your database through the monthly edifying corona magazines and quizzes conducted in the fests to hands-on activities like night observation, astrophotography,publishing papers,telescope building and more.In one word we can say that Astronomy club is supercalifragilistic expialidocious(extraordinarily good;wonderful “the only word to characterize Kepler’s discoveries”)Don’t missout to witness the extravaganza of the Astronomy Club.





Workshops

  • The first workshop was held on 17th August, 2016 on the topics - History of Astronomy, Extraterrestrial Life, Interstellar Travel and Telescopes.
  • The second workshop was held on 5th September, 2016 on the topics - Origin of the Universe, Big Bang Theory and Dark Energy and Dark Matter.
  • The third workshop was held on 21st September, 2016 on the topics - Basics of Telescopes and Types of Telescopes.
  • The sixth workshop was held on 11th January, 2017 on "Galaxies".
